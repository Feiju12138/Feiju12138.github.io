Print Text

<x>, <y>: Coordinates.
<font>: Font size ranging from 1 to 8, with 1 as the smallest and 8 as the largest.
<rotation>: Rotation angle, with values of 0, 90, 180, and 270.
<x-multiplication>, <y-multiplication>: Width-to-length ratio, with each value ranging from 1 to 10.
<content>: Text content.

<alignment>: Alignment.

1: Left alignment
2: Center alignment
3: Right alignment

Print Barcode

<x>, <y>: Coordinates.
<type>: Barcode type, such as 128, EAN128, EAN13, etc.
<height>: Barcode height.
<human-readable>: Alignment of the numbers at the bottom of the barcode.

0: Hide
1: Left alignment
2: Center alignment
3: Right alignment

<rotation>: Rotation angle, with values of 0, 90, 180, and 270.
<narrow>: Narrow element width.
<wide>: Wide element width.
<content>: Barcode content.

<alignment>: Alignment.

1: Left alignment
2: Center alignment
3: Right alignment

Print QR Code

<x>, <y>: Coordinates.
<ecc_level>: Error correction level.

L: 7%
M: 15%
Q: 25%
H: 30%

<cell_width>: QR code width, ranging from 1 to 10.
<auto>: Automatic encoding.

A: Automatic
M: Manual

<rotation>: Rotation angle, with values of 0, 90, 180, and 270.
<content>: QR code content.

Print Rectangular Frame

<x_start>, <y_start>: Coordinates of the top left corner of the frame.
<x_end>, <y_end>: Coordinates of the bottom right corner of the frame.
<width>: Thickness of the frame in dots.

<radius>: Radius of the rounded corners.
