Introduction

DOS, short for Disk Operating System, is a type of operating system for personal computers. From 1981 to 1995, DOS held a significant position in the IBM PC compatible market. Additionally, if we include some of the Microsoft Windows versions based on DOS, such as Windows 95, 98, and Me, its commercial life can be extended to at least the year 2000. (Wikipedia)
